About

I realised most people find it hard to spell my name, so I decided to go with something different for my domain name. To be honest, it didn't involve much creativity. I just happened to be eating some home-made food with a lot of delicious mushrooms in it while purchasing my domain name. You can see how the name came about from there.

I'm a self-taught web developer. I started my learning journey on Codecademy and any YouTube tutorials I could get my hands on and quickly fell in love with coding. The feeling of creating something out of nothing cannot be described with words only. It definitely beats the satisfaction I got from my Quantity Surveying and Marketing background.

I also took two courses on Udemy to get deeper insight into the industry. A web design course, which gave me a good foundation on Photoshop CC, and a web developer bootcamp. I completed the front-end part of the course and put what I have learnt to practice in this portfolio.
